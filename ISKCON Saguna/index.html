Welcome to Saguna Namhatta (ISKCON)

Our Mission

    Saguna Namhatta (ISKCON) is dedicated to spreading Krishna consciousness through devotional service, scriptural education, and congregational chanting of the holy names. Our mission is to inspire people to live a spiritually enriched life based on the principles of **Bhagavad Gita** and **Srimad Bhagavatam**, fostering universal love and harmony.

    Saguna Namhatta (ISKCON) is guided by our **respected Temple President**, who provides spiritual direction and ensures all activities align with the teachings of Srila Prabhupada.
