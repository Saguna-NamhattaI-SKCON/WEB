    Weekly Kirtan

    Join us every Wednesday evening for our weekly Kirtan session, where we come together to chant the holy names of Lord Krishna in a joyous and uplifting atmosphere.

    Event Details

    • Day: Every Wednesday
    • Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
    • Location: ISKCON Raghunathpur Temple

    What to Expect

    Our Kirtan sessions are a time for devotees to come together, sing, dance, and immerse in the divine vibrations of Lord Krishna's holy names. Whether you're a regular participant or new to the practice, everyone is welcome!
