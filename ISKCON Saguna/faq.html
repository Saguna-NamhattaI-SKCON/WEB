Frequently Asked Questions

What is Saguna Namhatta (ISKCON) ?

Saguna Namhatta (ISKCON) is a center dedicated to spreading the teachings of Lord Krishna as presented in the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam.

How can I participate in events?

You can visit our Events page for a complete list of upcoming programs and register for participation.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to ISKCON Raghunathpur are eligible for tax deductions under Section 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act.

How do I volunteer for temple activities?

You can contact us through our Contact page, and we will guide you on how to get involved as a volunteer.
